Classic Room

A peaceful National Park garden view from your white balcony table is one of the best features of our Classic rooms. Interior white walls are complemented by dark wood furniture, muted colours and contemporary clean lines for a fresh look and feel.

Alternatively, for those seeking a cozy haven indoors, our classic room without a balcony features large windows that invite ample natural light, offering glimpses of the tranquil views of the surrounding area.
